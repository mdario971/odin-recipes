Lasagna

Lasagna is a typical Italian dish made of pasta,Ragù Bolognese and besciamella.

The ingredients are put in altenate layers with on top Besciamella and Parmigiano.

Ingredients

  • Pasta for lasagna
  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Parmigiano

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ragù sauce cooking meat with the tomato sauce and let it slow cook.
  2. Cut Mozzarella cheese in little square
  3. Put the milk in a pot and add the nutmeg, salt and pepper. Turn on the cooker and add the flour mixing the milk until boil and the Besciamella look enough dense.
  4. Take a rectangular pot and put Besciamella on the bottom. Now start alternate layers of pasta, Ragu and Besciamella.
  5. When the pasta is over put parmigiano on the top layer.
  6. Now put the pot in oven and let the Lasagna cook for 2 hours at 200° C
